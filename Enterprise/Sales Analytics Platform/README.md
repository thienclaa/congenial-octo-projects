# Sales Analytics Platform

# Overview

Designed and maintained an enterprise Sales Analytics Platform supporting Pharmacy & Healthcare E-Commerce operations at FPT Retail – Long Châu.

The platform integrates sales transactions, promotions, payment methods, and customer behavior into executive Power BI dashboards, enabling data-driven decision-making across E-Commerce, Operations, and Management teams.

The solution processes approximately 100K+ daily sales transactions and supports more than 100 business users.

## Business Problem

The business required a centralized reporting platform capable of consolidating sales transactions, promotions, payment methods, and customer purchasing behavior from multiple data sources.

Existing reporting processes were fragmented, time-consuming, and unable to provide timely operational insights for executive decision-making.

## Solution

Developed an enterprise analytics platform using SQL Server, Azure Synapse Analytics, Databricks, Power BI Dataflows, and Semantic Models.

The platform delivers:

- Executive sales dashboards
- Sales KPI monitoring
- Promotion analytics
- Payment analytics
- Customer behavior analytics
- Product performance analysis
- Daily operational reporting

Data pipelines were orchestrated through Python workflows to ensure reliable daily refreshes.

## Business Impact

- Supported analytics for approximately 100K+ daily sales transactions.
- Built and maintained one of the company's primary executive sales dashboards.
- Served more than 100 business users across multiple business functions.
- Increased call center revenue by 15% through customer repurchase analytics.
- Reduced semantic model refresh time from 90 minutes to 30 minutes.
- Reduced Power BI Dataflow refresh time from 3 hours to 1.5 hours.
- Reduced semantic model storage from 15 GB to 5 GB.
